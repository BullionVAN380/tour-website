About-Us

Africa is a continent inspired by Adventure and Emotion; a continent rich with culture, unimaginable wildlife experiences and some of the most breathtaking sceneries. At Duruma Tours & Safaris, our focus is mainly concentrated on offering high quality safaris experiences within East Africa and in particular Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda and Uganda. Africa is much more than our passion, it is our home and our goal is to showcase the very best of it, to you as our guests. KARIBU SANA! Duruma Tours and Safaris was established in the year 2022 by qualified professionals with experience in the tourism industry.
